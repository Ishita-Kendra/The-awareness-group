Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)

Making TAG visible when buyers ask ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini — before they ever visit a website.

The concern raised: When someone searches "commercial solar financing" or "solar PPA without credit check" on AI engines, TAG doesn't appear. Competitors who get cited first own the conversation before it even begins.

Our position: AEO is a mentions and citations problem — not a separate technology problem. Roughly 80% of the solution sits inside our existing scope (content, schema markup, forum seeding, directories). The remaining 20% (deep PR placements, paid review-site listings) is an option we'll revisit at Month 6 once the foundation is built.

How AI Engines Source Their Answers
Training data — Reddit threads, Quora answers, Wikipedia, industry forums, blog posts crawled before the model's cutoff
Live web search — newer models (Perplexity, ChatGPT with Browse) pull from Bing/Google indexes in real time
Third-party trust signals — "best of" listicles, industry directories, comparison pages, earned media citations
The goal: TAG must appear on the sources AI trusts. Not just TAG's own website — but Reddit, Quora, SEIA directories, Solar Power World, and comparison articles that LLMs cite.
Real Target Prompts We'll Optimise For
  • "Best commercial solar financing companies in the United States"
  • "Solar PPA without credit check"
  • "How to finance solar installation with bad credit"
  • "Solar dealer programs with financing support"
  • "Solar broker commission programs USA"
  • "No FICO solar program residential"
  • "Commercial solar no upfront cost"
  • "Solar EPC financing partner"
Each prompt run 3× across ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini monthly to account for model non-determinism.
The Five-Layer AEO Approach
1

AEO-Shaped Content on TAG's Website

~30% of monthly Emplibot output reformatted as comparison pieces ("TAG vs Sunrun vs SunPower for commercial financing"), explainers ("How does a No-FICO PPA work?"), and Q&A formats. These are the exact formats LLMs cite. Same tool — different brief.

✓ In Scope — Month 2 onwards
2

Schema Markup — Technical Setup

FAQ schema, Article schema, and Organization schema added to TAG's website. Helps LLMs correctly parse, attribute, and cite content. One-time setup — configured during Day 11 technical audit.

✓ In Scope — Day 11
3

Reddit / Quora / Forum Seeding

2–3 high-quality, expertise-first answers per week on threads where solar buyers research. Subreddits: r/solar, r/solarenergy, r/Entrepreneur. Quora topics: solar financing, commercial solar. Real accounts with reputation — not promotional spam. Seed TAG's proof points: No-FICO PPA, 10% guaranteed savings, 26M homes, 642 projects.

✓ In Scope — Month 2 onwards
4

Industry Directories & Wikipedia

Submit TAG to SEIA member directory, EnergySage, Solar Power World listings, and other high-authority industry sources. Wikipedia entry created or expanded following notability and neutrality guidelines. These are permanent, high-trust citations.

✓ In Scope — Months 2–4
5

Earned Media — Press Coverage

Outbound to journalists at Solar Power World, PV Magazine, Greentech Media. TAG quoted in industry publications. Slower channel — builds compounding authority. Foundation work starts Months 5–8. If gaps remain at Month 6 review, a specialist PR firm is the targeted recommendation — not before.

✓ In Scope — Months 5–8 ⚡ PR Specialist option at M6
