How It Works — Detail

Why AEO matters now

AI engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini) don't browse in real time for most queries — they answer from training data (Reddit, Quora, Wikipedia, blogs), live web search (Bing/Google index), and third-party trust signals (comparison pages, directories, earned media). TAG's own website ranking is not enough. TAG must appear on the sources LLMs trust.

What's in scope (80%)

Schema markup, AEO-shaped content (comparison pages, explainers, Q&A formats), Reddit/Quora seeding, industry directories, Wikipedia, earned media outreach, and monthly/quarterly measurement — all fit inside the existing engagement. No new vendors required through Month 6.

The 20% — optional, assessed at Month 6

Deep PR placements in major trade publications and paid review-site listings (e.g., "Top 10 Solar Financing Companies 2026" — ~$500–2,000 one-time per placement) sit outside standard content scope. Evaluated at the Month 6 AI visibility audit. If a specific gap remains and a specialist PR firm would close it faster, that recommendation is made then — not before.
